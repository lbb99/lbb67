Aranya kand

In exile, Rāma, Sītā, and Laksmana journey southward along the banks of the river Godāvari, where they build cottages and live off the land. One day, in the Pañcavati forest they are visited by a rāklasī named Śurpanakhā, sister of Ravana. She tries to seduce the brothers and, after failing, attempts to kill Sītā out of jealousy. Laksmana stops her by cutting off her nose and ears. Hearing of this, her brothers Khara and Dushan organize an attack against the princes. Rama defeats Khara and his rakshasas.
