Ramayana the epic story

The ramayana written by Tulsidas known as Ramacharitmas, is a sixteemnth century epic. The story revolves over Rama, who was the seventh avatar of the Hindu God Vishnu. He is the central figure of the Hindu epic. Ramayana , which is the principal narrations of the events connected to his ideals and his greatness. He was thew eldest son of King Dashratha- the ruler of Koasla Kingdom. Rama's life and journey is one of adherenceof dharma despite harsh tests and obstaclesd and many pains of life and time. This adherence made him earn the name of Maryadas Purshottam which literally means The Perfect Man or Land of Self-Control or Lord of Virtue.
