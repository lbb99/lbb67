Makar Sankranti

Makar Sankranti is a Hindu festival celebrated in January. It marks the entrance of the sun into the astrological sign of makara (corresponding to Capricorn) and the beginning of the sun’s uttarayana (northward journey). The festival is a time to thank nature for its abundant resources and is especially significant for farmers as it marks the beginning of the harvest season. People celebrate by taking holy baths in rivers and paying tribute to the sun god (Surya).
